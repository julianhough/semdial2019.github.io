About SemDial

The annual SemDial Workshops aim at bringing together researchers working on the semantics and pragmatics of dialogue in fields as diverse as formal semantics and pragmatics, artificial intelligence, computational linguistics, psychology, and neural science.

In 2019, the 23rd edition of the SemDial Workshop Series will be hosted by the Cognitive Science Group (CogSci) at Queen Mary University of London, UK.

We invite submissions on all topics related to the semantics and pragmatics of dialogue. For more information about the scope, topic and format of the workshop please refer to our official Call for papers and our Submission Site for instructions on how to prepare and submit articles for presentation at SemdDial 2019 (LondonLogue).

SemDial 2019 called LondonLogue

by Janosch Haber/January 10, 2019

The 23rd edition of the SemDial Workshop Series will be organised by the Cognitive Science Group (CogSci) at Queen Mary University of London.

This makes it the second time that the Workshop will be held in the UK's capital. In 2008 the 12th annual SemDial conference took place at King's College London under the monniker LonDial.

Following naming tradition, this year's edition will therefore be called LondonLogue. The conference will take place September 4-6th, 2019 at Queen Mary's Mile End Campus.
