Sophie Scott

Professor of Cognitive Neuroscience - University College London (UCL)

Sophie Scott is a Professor of Cognitive Neuroscience at University College London (UCL) and head of the Speech Communication Lab. Her research investigates the cognitive neuroscience of voices, speech and laughter particularly speech perception, speech production, vocal emotions and human communication. She also serves as Deputy Director of UCL's Institute of Cognitive Neuroscience.
